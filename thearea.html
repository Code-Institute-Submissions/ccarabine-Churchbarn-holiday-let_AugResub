Perranuthnoe sits on Mounts Bay, a stunning stretch of coast, home to some of the area’s most iconic beaches, seaside towns, walks and attractions. The bay stretches from Lizard Point to Gwennap Head on the English Channel coast. Aside from plenty to do to fill your days, there are numerous restaurants, eateries, and pubs, providing a plethora of options to refresh and fuel, catering to all budgets and tastes.
