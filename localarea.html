Set on Mounts Bay, in the unspoilt seaside village of Perranuthnoe, this lovingly converted barn is a prime coastal perch. With showstopping sunsets over Cudden Point, the area's iconic beaches are easily accessible. Suitable for families or couples who return to the area year after year, the exceptionally equipped cool, clean and airy interiors and secluded sub-tropical garden is the perfect hideaway to reset and recharge.
